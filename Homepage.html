About Us

Since its founding back in 2012 by Nancy Pinchot and Yosil Foigelman, and known first as "Music at Grey Towers", Kindred Spirits Arts Programs (KSAP) has taken inspiration from Asher Durand's iconic painting of the same name and has explored the relationship between the beauty of nature and the creation of art both musical and visual.
